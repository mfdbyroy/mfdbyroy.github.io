The Monochrome Productions was established in August 2015 as part of a re-management project for Ministry of Film & Design.

 

The Monochrome is used to solely handle Video Editing Projects, projects are handle in a way that the most time needed to to finish the project will have the most priority.

 

Videos are done to the requirement of the client & I might add might my own little spin on it, well it really depends on the footages that you gave me.

Projects are done for Free.
