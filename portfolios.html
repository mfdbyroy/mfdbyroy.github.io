Ministry of Film & Design was established in April 2015 as part of a Portfolio Building Project. Ministry of Film & Design does Designing & Video Editing for now. All Projects will be done for free (as of now).

 

This Portfolio is managed under 1 person. There are 2 other spin-off portfolio that are listed below, they are used as a part of a management system. Projects done by the 2 Portfolio will still be under Ministry of Film & Design.
